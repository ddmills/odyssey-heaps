## Crew

Each member of your crew has:

- A die, this is used in combat and various decisions
- A healthbar, used in combat
- A nationality
- A positive trait
- A negative trait


Ship Roles

- Navigator
- Lookout
- Cook
- Carpenter
- Boatswain (aka bosun) (sails)
- Quartermaster
- Parson (chaplain, clergy)
- Purser ($)
- Doctor
- Deckhand
- Gunner
- Powder Monkey
